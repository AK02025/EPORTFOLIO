Learn more about me

Say something about
yourself, short and sweet.

Field-level PM on a $300M+ Mumbai wastewater facility. IT/cybersecurity PMO. Animation & VFX undergrad turned BA candidate at Northeastern. I close the gap between strategy and execution — and I build things that actually ship.

My path to PM is genuinely non-linear. I started in Animation & VFX at Amity University Mumbai — four years learning to see systems, communicate visually, and think in both structure and story. That lens has been one of my most underrated PM assets.

A year managing a $300M+ municipal infrastructure project in Mumbai gave me field-level instincts no classroom replicates: coordinating 3 government regulators, managing 4 subcontractors, tracking 40+ FMRs, holding 100% schedule adherence where delays cost real money.

At L.A. Technologies I moved into IT/cybersecurity PMO — building Power BI dashboards, Jira risk registers, coordinating a Juniper firewall migration across 4 sites with zero downtime. Then Boston, Northeastern, MS in PM with a BA concentration.

The Dunkin' app started as a work problem I couldn't stop thinking about. ScopeCheck too. That is how I know an idea is worth building.
